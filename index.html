About Us

Who we are and What we do

We are a young non-profit registered organisation that was founded in 1st January 2020 and our aim is to raise funds to provide a permanent solution to self sustainability in world slums.
We provide food, education initiatives, essentials and disaster response as we help children and their families be independent and self-reliant,
we are not oly focused on providing basic need to the needy but also naturing talents of youth in the slums to reduce the level of joblesness and increase security. Our students are also give scholarship to the unversity level.

Our Mission

Our mission is to establish hope in the hearts and change lives of people all over the slum and remort areas.

Our objective

To Assist in the development of gifted children and capable young people by providing equal opportunities based exclusively on their professional skills and sponsoring their education, professional training and creative development;

Donate

For every amount contributed monthly, a family averaging five members gets an EMPOWER PACK with foodstuff that can last a month, gets to hear the gospel of hope in Jesus and gets help with setting them up to be self sustainable.
